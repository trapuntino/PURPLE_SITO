What's all about

Purple Studio is a Mountain-based creative house founded by Fabrizio Confortola and Jacopo Perotti, blending the artistry of design with the dynamism of video production. Fabrizio, a graphic designer with a degree from Politecnico di Milano, brings a meticulous approach to visual identity and brand design, while Jacopo, a video maker and producer with a background from IED Milano, captures the energy of storytelling through cinematic visuals.

Where vision and creativity meet to bring your brand to life.

At Purple Studio, we are passionate about creating visuals that stand out and communicate powerfully. From brand identities and graphic concepts to video storytelling and production, we collaborate closely with our clients to transform ideas into impactful, polished creations. Driven by creativity and grounded in design principles, our team offers a range of services to elevate brands and projects to the next level.
