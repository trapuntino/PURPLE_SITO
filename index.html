Welcome to Purple Studio

Purple Studio is a Milan-based creative studio founded by Fabrizio Confortola and Jacopo Perotti, blending the expertise of graphic design and video production to craft bold visual experiences. Our work reflects our founders' unique backgrounds: Fabrizio, a graphic designer with a degree from Politecnico di Milano, brings a refined eye for design and brand identity; Jacopo, a talented video maker and producer with a degree from IED Milano, channels his storytelling abilities into dynamic, cinematic content.
